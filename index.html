ABOUT

Meet Dr. Sumit Sural

“Thorough physical assessment and clinical judgment are the foundation of effective treatment.”

A highly experienced Orthopaedic Surgeon & Professor committed to delivering exceptional patient care through accurate diagnosis and evidence-based treatment.

Dr. Sumit Sural is a Professor of Orthopaedics at Maulana Azad Medical College (MAMC) and LNJP Hospital, Delhi. He completed his medical education from the prestigious University College of Medical Sciences (UCMS).

With over a decade of experience, he specializes in managing complex orthopaedic conditions with a strong emphasis on clinical examination and patient-centered care.
